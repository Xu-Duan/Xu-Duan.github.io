I am currently a Research Assitant with Rig Automation and Performance Improvement in Drilling (RAPID) which is an industry affiliate program at UT Austin’s Cockrell School of Engineering, aiming at building advanced multi-phase fluid model. I am also pursuing a Master’s degree in Mechanical Engineering at The University of Texas at Austin, focusing on Dynamic Systems and Control. I was born in Bozhou, Anhui, China in 2002.

My studies are focused on Computational Science. I'm also interested in Machine Learning and Numerical Analysis.

I would describe myself as a enthusiastic and curious person, always looking to new problems to solve.
